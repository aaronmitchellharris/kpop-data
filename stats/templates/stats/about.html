Welcome to Kpop-Numbers!

 

I built this website as a tool for monitoring and visualizing Youtube music video views of popular Kpop Artists. It is still a work in progress, but I hope one day it will be seamless and easy to use for everyone. Please let me know if there are any bugs or improvements you would like to bring to my attention. Thank you!

 

 

Q&A:

 

How often are music video views updated?

The data for each music video is updated every midnight pacific time using Youtube's api

 

I don't see my favorite artist. Will you be adding more?

Contact me with any missing/incorrect artists or information on this website

 

How is this tool useful?

By visualizing music video views, it is easier to see trends or correlations with popularity. I hope to add explanations for the graphs and charts in the future to help visitors understand what they are seeing. Additionally, I plan to apply data analytics to make predictions about a group's future popularity based on multiple of factors.
